Literature/Book Clubs


Poppy Loves Book Club

Poppy Loves Book club members make up the most incredible and welcoming community of women around the world, who discovered them through their love of books and have gone on to forge real friendships and make special and important connections, all through their online book club. Each month they read wonderful, thought provoking books together, attend online inspiring events, they chat, support each other, do yoga, watch movies, drink coffe and so much more...


Ladies Lit Squad

Ladies Lit Squad. is an all female book club and community sharing reads by women and authors of colour. Check their reading lists for recommendations they make on their Instagram page @ladieslitsquad to support independent bookshops.



Badasss Women's Book Club

Badasss Women's Book Club is a community of women who support each other on the journey of being their most empowered, authenitc and confident self. Through intentional discussions, meaninful connections and engaging resources, they inspire women to take action and live their badass life.



The Girly Book Club

The Girly Book Club is a global book club for likeminded women to discuss great books! All members of the book club read the same book over the same period; members then meet up in their respective cities at the end of each month to discuss the book and exchange views. This forum provides a rare and relaxed atmosphere for women to meet other like-minded women over a glass of wine and share a common passion.
